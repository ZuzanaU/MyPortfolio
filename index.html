Ahoj [Hey], I'm Zuzana

My name is Zuzana Urbanova and I am a Design enthusiast, currently employed as UX/UI designer in the biggest Slovak bank. I have been working on many awesome projects going from customer research, wireframing to the creation the final UI. Below I am presenting a selection of my side projects.

How to find a Parking spot in Melbourne?

Within a bootcamp called Butterfly effect I joined the team that have created the Melberry App. The App's purpose is to help users find parking easier and faster. You should only open the app and see available parking spots. The App laverages on open data and IOT technologies. Would you like to learn more about Melberry App?

Web page for RoBin an intelligent trash bin

RoBin is an open source project focusing on improving trash collection and sorting as well as demonstrating the utility of AI for ecological purposes. I have worked on its webpage including all its illustrations. Second part of my job here was to create a logo: representing the combination of ecology and technology. Learn more about Robin at its webpage.

Let’s play, LET’S LEARN

Flashcards! is a free and open source PWA flash card App. I have contributed on the Process flow and Design. You can find out more about the project on GitHub.
